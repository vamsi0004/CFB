About Us

Crime Free Bharat (श्रेष्ठ, आत्मनिर्भर, अपराध मुक्ति संस्था) is a national initiative led by Maithili Sharan Gupta IPS, the former DGP of Police Reforms and MP. Our mission is to reduce crimes to a drastic minimum in India through a five-pronged strategy.

  • Certainty of registration of FIR
  • Certainty of Detection with 21 technologies
  • Certainty of Conviction with digital and non-digital evidence
  • Certainty of Prevention through police and community volunteers
  • Certainty of Rehabilitation of criminals
